# v0.6 Feature Spec — Reflections System

## Purpose

Support **metacognitive awareness** by prompting students to reflect on
their thinking patterns, connections, and uncertainties.

Reflections are **not performance artifacts**.

---

## Core Concept

Reflection is a *conversation*, not a submission.

AI prompts reflection but does not judge it.

---

## Student Experience

- Periodic reflection invitations (e.g., end of unit)
- Chat-style interview interface
- Prompts such as:
  - What ideas have returned most often?
  - Which connections surprised you?
  - What still feels unresolved?
- Student responses are editable
- Final reflection saved as a private document

---

## Teacher Experience

- Teachers can read reflections
- Used for:
  - understanding student thinking
  - identifying confusion or growth
- Reflections are contextual, not evidentiary

---

## Assessment

- No scoring
- Optional participation credit only
- Reflections do not affect unlocks

---

## AI Role

- Ask probing questions
- Encourage specificity
- Never suggest “better” answers
- Never assess quality

---

## Design Constraints

- No grading rubric
- No comparative analytics
- No automated summaries presented to students
