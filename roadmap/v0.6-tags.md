# v0.6 Feature Spec — Tags System

## Purpose

Provide a **secondary, optional layer of organization**
to support discovery and reflection.

Tags do not replace [[linking]].

---

## Student Experience

- Add tags using #syntax in notes
- View notes by tag
- Browse tag clusters

---

## Teacher Experience

- See which tags are emerging
- Identify thematic patterns
- No evaluation of tag “quality”

---

## AI Role (Optional)

- Suggest tags during note creation
- Student may accept or ignore suggestions

---

## Design Constraints

- No points for tagging
- Tags do not affect unlocks
- Tags are supplemental, not primary
